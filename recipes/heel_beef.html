Ingredients

  • 1kg and 1/2 of Heel Beef
  • 1 cup of white wine
  • 2 big onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2-3 garlic gloves
  • 4 potatos
  • 500ml of meath broth
  • 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the disc over medium heat and add 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil. Place the ossobuco on the disc and sear on both sides until the meat is browned, approximately 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and reserve.
  2. Add a little more oil if necessary. Add the onions, bell pepper, carrot and garlic. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are golden brown.
  3. Put the osso buco back on the disk. Deglaze with the white wine and let the alcohol evaporate for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato preserve and mix well. Then, add the meat broth, ensuring that the liquid partially covers the meat and vegetables.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, rosemary and bay leaf to taste. Cover the disc and cook over low heat for 1.5 to 2 hours, making sure it does not run out of liquid.
  6. After 30 minutes of cooking, add the sliced ​​potatoes around the meat. Cover again and continue cooking until the potatoes are tender, about 45-60 minutes more.
